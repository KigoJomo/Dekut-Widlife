naro-Moru tree planting

nov 13 2023

naro-Moru level-4 hospital | naro-Moru police station

DEKUWEC joined hands with the GOGreenHub Club of Nyeri on November 13, 2023, for a tree planting event at Naro~Moru. The club's partnership with the Kenya Forest Club of Nyeri was pivotal in making this possible. Committed to the '15 Billion Trees Goal,' DEKUWEC extends gratitude to all stakeholders involved, especially the Keya Forest Club and The GoGreenHub of Nyeri.
